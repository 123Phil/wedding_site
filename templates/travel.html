    Local directions:

    From Gaffey street you can turn right onto Leavenworth drive and then left into the main parking lot, or take the next right directly into the main parking lot. The ceremony will be held on the south side of the Bell.

    The reception will be held at the Angel's Gate Cultural Center, Bldg H. To get there from the main parking lot, take the back exit and turn left up Leary Merriam road, then turn left at the fork and stay left until you pass Bldg H. The parking lot for the reception is a dirt and gravel lot on the west side of Bldg H.
